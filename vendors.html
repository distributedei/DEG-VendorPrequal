Before You Start

The prequalification process takes approximately 15–20 minutes. Please have the following ready before you begin:

  • ✓Federal Tax ID (EIN)
  • ✓General Liability & Workers Comp information
  • ✓Current Certificate of Insurance with expiration date
  • ✓EMR — Experience Modification Rate (current + 2 prior years)
  • ✓W-9 document (PDF upload required)
  • ✓Sample insurance certificate (PDF upload required)
  • ✓OSHA 300 logs — past 3 years
  • ✓At least two trade references (GC or project owner)
  • ✓Executive & operations contact information
  • ✓Relevant licenses & certifications
If you don't have an executed NDA with Distributed Energy, you'll be able to download our NDA template during the application. Please have it signed and ready to upload.

Your progress cannot be saved — please complete the form in one session.
